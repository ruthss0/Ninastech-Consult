What We Do?

Ninastech help clients improve their operations, increase efficiency, and drive growth through the use of technology.

  • Software Development

    We begin by understanding our client's business objectives and identifying their software development needs. Using agile methodologies, we then develop custom software solutions and provide regular updates to the client throughout the development process.

  • Project Management

    Our expert project management and delivery services ensure that your projects are completed on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards. We can help you manage every aspect of your project, from planning and execution to monitoring and control.

  • Quality Assurance and Testing

    Our software testing and quality assurance services ensure that your software solutions meet the highest quality standards. Our experienced team can help you with all aspects of software testing, from planning and execution to analysis and reporting.

  • Mobile App development

    Our mobile app development services include iOS and Android app development, to help you reach your target audience on any device. Ninastech can help you bring your mobile app idea to life, from concept and design to development and launch. Let us help you develop a mobile app that delivers a great user experience and drives business success.

  • UX & UI Design

    Ninastech can help you with every aspect of UX design, from user research and user interface (UI) design to usability testing. We can help you create a visually appealing design that are intuitive, accessible, and engaging. We can help you improve user satisfaction, reduce development costs, and drive business growth.

  • Ninastech Consulting

    Advance your career and become an expert software engineer consultant with our creative and practical course. Learn from industry professionals and gain hands-on experience with the latest tools and techniques in software engineering consulting. Our course is designed to help you develop the skills and knowledge you need to succeed in this dynamic field.

Why Work With Us?

We work as a team of mentor volunteers that can help you until you are confident to get the job!

  • Volunteering will provide you with a space to learn, make mistakes, improve and grow. Is a very rich opportunity to learn new skills and also teach others around you. Companies look for years of experience, the longer you work in the field, the more chances you get to find an interview opportunity.

  • Volunteering gives you the opportunity to learn more about the tasks of that position. Find developers to collaborate with and make new friends from other countries.

  • It's an incredible opportunity to work with people of different cultures and make connections. With these experiences, as you progress in your career, you will be able to tell the stories of these learnings in interviews and also use these skills in industry positions in USA.
