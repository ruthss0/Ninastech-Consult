What We Do?

Ninastech help and influence girls to work in the technology field.

  • Web Developer Course

    We offer a free web developer beginner course. Which will be fundamental for your path in the area of technology.(HTML,CSS,Javascript,Design with Figma, Node.js)

  • Mental Health

    We also offer psychological support for working with technology.It is not enough to have knowledge or ability to learn programming, you need resilience, perseverance and believing in yourself.

  • Social Media Course

    Growth and your career by learning how to: build an effective social media strategy, set up social listening and moderation, create social content, extend your reach, digital advertising, and more.

  • Book Club

    In book-club, is the read and discuss book group for ninas. Everyone is welcome to join in the book discussion! Bookenders will meet at discord.

  • Code Challenges

    The coding challenges in this chanell at discord that are intended for ninas, therefore the solutions are usualy implemented using only simpleclassical programming elements.

  • Create Your Business Website

    We are a consulting software company that helps companies build their business websites. We provide technical expertise and offer strategic guidance to help clients leverage technology to achieve their business goals, including advice on digital transformation and related technologies.

  • NinasTalk

    Almost ever Sunday we have meetings with students or peolple that work in tech. They inspire us with informations and storys about careers. And you can also share your experience!

  • English Speakers

    Free nice conversations in English with peolple from other country in the world, training for interviews and read english books about code together.Expand your career!

Why Work With Us?

We work as a team of mentor volunteers that can help you until you are confident to get the job!

  • Volunteering will provide you with a space to learn, make mistakes, improve and grow. Is a very rich opportunity to learn new skills and also teach others around you. Companies look for years of experience, the longer you work in the field, the more chances you get to find an interview opportunity.

  • Volunteering gives you the opportunity to learn more about the tasks of that position. Find developers to collaborate with and make new friends from other countries.

  • It's an incredible opportunity to work with people of different cultures and make connections. With these experiences, as you progress in your career, you will be able to tell the stories of these learnings in interviews and also use these skills in industry positions in USA.
